Course Description

This comprehensive course takes you from Arduino basics to building fully functional robots. Through hands-on projects, you'll master essential robotics concepts while gaining practical skills in programming, electronics, and mechanical design.

Each module builds on the previous one, starting with simple circuits and progressing to autonomous robots with advanced sensors. By the end, you'll have the confidence to design and build your own Arduino-based robotic projects.

No prior experience required! We start from the absolute basics.

About Your Instructor

Dr. Michael Rodriguez is a robotics engineer with over a decade of experience in both industry and academia. After completing his PhD at Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Institute, he worked on robotic systems for NASA and several tech startups before transitioning to education.

Michael specializes in making complex robotics concepts accessible to beginners. His teaching philosophy emphasizes hands-on learning through practical projects that reinforce theoretical concepts. He has taught Arduino and robotics to over 5,000 students worldwide through online courses and workshops.

When he's not teaching, Michael develops open-source robotics projects and contributes to the Arduino community. His work has been featured in Make Magazine, Hackaday, and at several robotics conferences.
