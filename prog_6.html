Course Description

This advanced course combines robotics and artificial intelligence using the Robot Operating System (ROS). You'll learn to integrate cutting-edge AI techniques like computer vision, deep learning, and reinforcement learning with robotic systems to create intelligent, adaptive behaviors.

Through practical projects, you'll implement neural networks in ROS, process sensor data with AI models, and develop robots that can perceive, learn, and make autonomous decisions. By the end, you'll be equipped to build the next generation of AI-powered robotic applications.

AI Concepts You'll Master

Computer Vision

Implement object detection, recognition, and tracking using OpenCV and deep learning models in ROS.

  • YOLO and SSD models
  • Real-time video processing
  • Depth perception

Deep Learning

Integrate neural networks with robotic systems for complex decision making and perception tasks.

  • PyTorch/TensorFlow in ROS
  • Transfer learning
  • Model optimization

Reinforcement Learning

Train robots to learn complex behaviors through trial and error in simulated environments.

  • Q-learning
  • Deep Q Networks
  • Policy gradients

Behavior Planning

Develop intelligent decision-making systems that combine AI models with traditional robotics.

  • State machines
  • Behavior trees
  • Hybrid architectures

AI Robotics Workflow

1

Data Collection

Gather sensor data from your robot's cameras, LIDAR, and other sensors to create training datasets.

2

Model Training

Train AI models using collected data or leverage pre-trained models with transfer learning.

3

ROS Integration

Package your AI models as ROS nodes that can process sensor data and output commands.

4

Deployment

Run your AI-powered ROS system on real robots or in simulation for testing and refinement.
